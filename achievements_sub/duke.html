Duke of Edingburghs Award

Last year in 2014 me and twenty plus other students from my school (and the fulham enterprise studio) took part on the DofE bronze expedition, before this we had to complete several other and equally important untits of the course, we had to organise voluentering, take part in extra fitness sessions (which first introduced me to weight-lifting) and gain an extra skill / talent. as well as this we also had to go on a two day practice assesment all of which certify the value of the DofE award.

The Award

After completing the different required units we were handed the certificate and badges as well as an opporunity to advance to silver the DofE.

The award itself has been handed out to students gobally for over 50 years now, it is seperated into 3 distinct stages of expenentially increasing difficulty, Bronze, Silver and Gold. This year I will be completing the expedition for silver.

In recent years the number of students taking the challenge has steadily increased, as the award becomes better recognised by more employers.
