2014 Science Exams

If it is not already evident, let me start by saying my passion for science is unparalleled. I've always been fascinated with the human body specifically with genetics and inheritec factors, howerver it was only until I reached year 7 (first year secondary education) that my interest and knowledge within the subject was broadened. Being placed in an accelerated tutor group because of my abilities, I quickly saw myself progress, surpassing the targets assigned to me several terms in advance.

Results

I chose tripple science as an option meaning by the end of year 11 I would have garnered 3 seperate GCSEs in science each one comprosed of 4 individual units: Biology, Chemistry, Physics and the ISA (coursework), an administrative decision in our school meant that I was able complete my first two science GCSEs a year in advance, I achieved an A* in all Biology, Chemistry and Physics exams for both core and additional science and an A* and an A respectively for the ISAs the end result was 6 individual A*s (in exams) an A* for for core science and an overall A for additional.
