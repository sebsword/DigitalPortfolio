ICT Course

I had intially chosen GCSE ICT as one of my main subjects option in year 9, however due to manegerial changes at my school my entire year group was put down for the BTEC ICT course.

We had planned to complete both a BTEC and a GCSE for ICT, but when our teacher the head of IT left in year 10, compromises has to be made. Regardless the vocational BTEC course has proven to be equally challenging and rewarding.

Main Aspects

  • Planning Stage (storyboard & timetables)
  • Justification of designs
  • Implementation of designs (using relatively advanced software)
  • Review of Final product

There is a certain stigma attatched to BTECs and the idea that they are targeted to less intelligent students but I would like to rebuke that statement, BTECs are equivelant to two GCSEs and are prefered by employers who recognise their value.
